[[person:brad-mehldau]]'s 2023 solo-piano album devoted to the Beatles songbook, recorded live at the Philharmonie de Paris in 2020. Ten Lennon/McCartney–Harrison songs plus a single non-Beatles closer, [[song:life-on-mars]] by [[person:david-bowie]]. The album sits alongside [[album:ride-into-the-sun]] as one of two single-songbook tribute records in his leader/co-leader discography.
