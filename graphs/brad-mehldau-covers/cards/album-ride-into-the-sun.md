[[person:brad-mehldau]]'s 2025 album dedicated almost entirely to [[person:elliott-smith]], with a single Nick Drake closer (*Sunday*) and one [[group:big-star]] cover (*Thirteen*). The companion to *Highway Rider*'s *Sky Turning Grey (For Elliott Smith)* and a culmination of two decades of Mehldau's engagement with Smith's music.

Related: [[person:brad-mehldau]], and [[person:elliott-smith]].
