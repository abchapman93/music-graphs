*Bass on Top* (1957, Blue Note) is [[person:paul-chambers|Paul Chambers]]'s third album as a leader, a quartet date with Hank Jones (piano), Kenny Burrell (guitar), and Art Taylor (drums). Recorded while Chambers was simultaneously anchoring the first Miles Davis Quintet, the album foregrounds his arco (bowed) playing alongside his deep-pocketed walking.
