*The Peaceful Side* (1961, United Artists) is [[person:billy-strayhorn|Billy Strayhorn]]'s only album released under his own name during his lifetime. Recorded in Paris with a small group including the Paris Blue Notes vocal quartet, the session features intimate, often whispered readings of Strayhorn's own compositions — "Lush Life", "Chelsea Bridge", "Passion Flower", "Lotus Blossom" — sung and played by the composer himself.
