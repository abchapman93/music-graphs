Marcus Miller is an American bassist, composer, and producer — a defining voice in contemporary jazz and a longtime collaborator of Miles Davis and David Sanborn. He toured with [[person:brett-williams|Brett Williams]] while Williams was in college, and appears on [[track:detroit|"Detroit"]] (alongside [[person:sean-jones|Sean Jones]]) and on [[track:be-kind|"Be Kind"]] by Williams's wife, [[person:kate-k-s|Kate K-S]].
