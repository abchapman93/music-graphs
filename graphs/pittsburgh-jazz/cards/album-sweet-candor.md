*Sweet Candor* (2015, MCG Jazz) is vocalist [[person:maureen-budway|Maureen Budway]]'s final recording, made shortly before her death from cancer. The set ranges from the Stephen Foster ballad "Hard Times Come Again No More" to a Gershwin medley and a bossa-nova reading of Jobim's "Águas de Março". [[person:sean-jones|Sean Jones]] sits in on trumpet for the second track, "Sweet Lover No More". The context of Budway's illness gives the program — especially "Hard Times" — an additional weight.
