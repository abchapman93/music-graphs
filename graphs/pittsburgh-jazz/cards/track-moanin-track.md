Bobby Timmons' "Moanin'" — the title track of [[person:art-blakey]] & The Jazz Messengers' 1958 [[album:moanin]] — is the defining hard-bop statement: a soul-blues piano vamp answered by gospel-call horns, with Blakey's press roll driving it home. Related: [[location:pittsburgh]].
