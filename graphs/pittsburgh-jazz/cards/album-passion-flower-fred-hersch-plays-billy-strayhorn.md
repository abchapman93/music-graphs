*Passion Flower: Fred Hersch Plays Billy Strayhorn* (1996, Nonesuch) is pianist Fred Hersch's solo-piano homage to [[person:billy-strayhorn|Billy Strayhorn]]. Hersch threads Strayhorn's well-known ballads — "Chelsea Bridge", "A Flower Is a Lovesome Thing", the title "Passion Flower" — with lesser-played pieces, treating each as a chamber-music subject rather than a vehicle for soloing.
