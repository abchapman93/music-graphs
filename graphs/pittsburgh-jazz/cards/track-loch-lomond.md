"Loch Lomond" is a Scottish folk ballad that became [[person:maxine-sullivan]]'s breakthrough 1937 hit, given a gently swinging Claude Thornhill arrangement that made the case for jazz-inflected reinterpretations of folk material. Related: [[location:pittsburgh]].
