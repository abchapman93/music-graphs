Dr. James T. Johnson Jr. (d. 2026) was a Pittsburgh pianist, educator, and co-founder — with his wife [[person:pamela-johnson|Pamela Johnson]] — of the [[group:afro-american-music-institute|Afro-American Music Institute]]. Father of drummer [[person:james-johnson-iii|James Johnson III]]. For four decades he taught generations of young Pittsburgh musicians through AAMI.
