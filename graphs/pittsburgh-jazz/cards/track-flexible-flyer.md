"Flexible Flyer" is a [[person:jimmy-mcgriff]] organ-trio cut emblematic of the soul-jazz Hammond B-3 vocabulary that connected [[location:pittsburgh]]'s organ-trio scene (Gene Ludwig, Sonny Clark sidemen) to the broader east-coast hard-bop lineage. Related: [[location:pittsburgh]].
