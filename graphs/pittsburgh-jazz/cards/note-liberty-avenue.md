**Composition note.** [[track:liberty-avenue-stroll]] is written by [[person:sean-jones]] and recorded by the [[group:mack-avenue-superband]]. The title refers to Liberty Avenue, a major thoroughfare in [[location:pittsburgh]] that runs from the Strip District through downtown and out into Bloomfield and Lawrenceville — not to be confused with **Wylie Avenue**, the storied corridor that ran through the [[note:hill-district]] and anchored the city's mid-20th-century jazz scene (see [[track:wylie-avenue-days]] by [[person:roger-humphries]] for the Hill District counterpart in this graph).
