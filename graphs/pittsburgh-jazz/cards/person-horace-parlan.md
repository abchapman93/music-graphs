Horace Parlan was a pianist born in [[location:pittsburgh]] in 1931 whose right hand was partially paralyzed by polio as a child — a constraint he turned into a percussive, bluesy, left-hand-driven style that defined his sound. He recorded a series of Blue Note dates as a leader in the early 1960s (*Us Three*, *Speakin' My Piece*) before emigrating to Denmark in 1972, where he continued recording into the 2010s.
