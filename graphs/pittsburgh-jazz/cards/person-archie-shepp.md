Archie Shepp is a tenor saxophonist born in Fort Lauderdale, Florida in 1937 and raised in Philadelphia, central to the 1960s free-jazz movement (with Cecil Taylor, John Coltrane, and on Impulse! as a leader). His Pittsburgh connection is through the Pittsburgh-rooted hard-bop lineage he engaged with as a player and the city's jazz programs where his music has been a frequent subject of study.
