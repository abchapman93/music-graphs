*Moanin'* is a 1959 album by [[person:art-blakey]] and the Jazz Messengers, recorded for Blue Note Records. With Bobby Timmons on piano, Lee Morgan on trumpet, Benny Golson on tenor saxophone, and Jymie Merritt on bass, the album is the canonical statement of the hard bop sound: bluesy, gospel-inflected melodies riding Blakey's thunderous drumming. The title track, written by Timmons with its iconic call-and-response head, became a jazz standard, as did Golson's "Blues March" and "Along Came Betty" from the same date.
