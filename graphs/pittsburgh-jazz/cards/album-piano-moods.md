*Piano Moods* (1955, Columbia) is a trio session by [[person:earl-hines|Earl "Fatha" Hines]] recorded near the tail end of his Columbia contract. The album showcases the trumpet-influenced single-note right-hand lines that earned Hines his "trumpet-style" reputation, paired with the rolling stride-derived left hand he developed in his early Pittsburgh and Chicago years.
