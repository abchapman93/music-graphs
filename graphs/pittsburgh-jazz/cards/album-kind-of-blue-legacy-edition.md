*Kind of Blue* (1959, Columbia; this Legacy Edition reissued with the 1958 Newport set as bonus material) is the Miles Davis Sextet album that opened jazz to modal improvisation. [[person:paul-chambers|Paul Chambers]] plays bass throughout, anchoring the floating modal harmonies of "So What", "Flamenco Sketches", and "All Blues" with his characteristic deep-centered tone.
