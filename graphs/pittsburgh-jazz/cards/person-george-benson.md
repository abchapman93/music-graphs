George Benson is a guitarist and vocalist born in [[location:pittsburgh]] in 1943 who came up playing in Hill District jazz clubs as a teenager before joining organist Jack McDuff's band in the early 1960s. After a decade as a hard-bop guitarist on Prestige and CTI, his 1976 album *Breezin'* crossed him over into pop-jazz stardom, winning multiple Grammys and establishing a template for smooth jazz.
