*Keep the Faith* (2011, Corona Records) is a leader date by Pittsburgh drummer [[person:roger-humphries|Roger Humphries]]. The session is a hometown affair — recorded in Pittsburgh with local players — and represents the post-Blue Note chapter of Humphries's long career as a bandleader and educator in the city's jazz scene.
