*Gene Harris Trio Plus One* (1993, Concord Jazz) pairs pianist Gene Harris and his trio (bassist [[person:ray-brown]], drummer Harold Jones) with tenor saxophonist [[person:stanley-turrentine]] as the fourth voice. The session is a relaxed blues-and-standards date that foregrounds Turrentine's warm, vocalized tenor sound against Harris's blues-soaked piano and Brown's anchoring bass.

Related: [[person:stanley-turrentine]], and [[person:ray-brown]].
