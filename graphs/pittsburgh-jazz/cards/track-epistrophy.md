"Epistrophy" is a Thelonious Monk composition co-credited to [[person:kenny-clarke]], who helped shape the tune's irregular angular structure during the early-1940s Minton's Playhouse sessions. Clarke's recorded versions emphasize the propulsive ride-cymbal feel that he pioneered. Related: [[location:pittsburgh]].
