"Mellow Mood" is a [[person:dodo-marmarosa]] composition and a centerpiece of his 1961 Argo album *Dodo's Back!*, the late-career return that documented his still-formidable bebop technique years after he had withdrawn from the scene. Related: [[location:pittsburgh]].
