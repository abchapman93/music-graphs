"Breezin'" is the title track of [[person:george-benson]]'s 1976 album of the same name, a Bobby Womack composition that became an unexpected crossover hit and set the template for the smooth-jazz genre. Benson's fluid single-line guitar runs anchor a lush, mid-tempo groove. Related: [[location:pittsburgh]].
