George Heid III is a Pittsburgh jazz drummer and a core member of [[group:elevations|Elevations]]. He is the son of [[person:george-heid-ii|George Heid II]], the influential Pittsburgh drummer and recording engineer. George Heid III wrote the track "Dad" on [[person:roger-humphries|Roger Humphries]]'s album [[album:keep-the-faith|Keep the Faith]], dedicated to his father.
