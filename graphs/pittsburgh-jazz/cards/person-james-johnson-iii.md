James Johnson III is a contemporary Pittsburgh-based drummer and composer leading his own quartet and quintet projects, working extensively across the city's modern post-bop scene. His leader records draw on the rhythmic vocabulary of Brian Blade and the Pittsburgh percussion lineage of [[person:art-blakey]] and [[person:roger-humphries]]. Related: [[location:pittsburgh]].

Son of [[person:dr-james-t-johnson-jr|Dr. James T. Johnson Jr.]] and [[person:pamela-johnson|Pamela Johnson]], co-founders of the [[group:afro-american-music-institute|Afro-American Music Institute]]. He toured with [[person:ahmad-jamal|Ahmad Jamal]] and released the leader album [[album:full-circle-album|Full Circle]].
