*Concert by the Sea* (1955, Columbia) is [[person:erroll-garner|Erroll Garner]]'s most famous album — a trio set recorded live at Sunset Auditorium in Carmel-by-the-Sea, California. The performance includes Garner's signature reading of his own composition [[track:misty]] in its now-canonical ballad treatment. Released without overdubs, the record captured Garner's percussive left-hand and behind-the-beat phrasing for a wide audience.
