Jimmy McGriff was a Hammond B-3 organist born in Philadelphia in 1936 whose 1962 hit "I've Got a Woman" helped define the soul-jazz organ-trio sound. His Pittsburgh connection is through the shared organ-trio circuit he worked alongside [[person:gene-ludwig]] and the broader east-coast hard-bop scene that ran through [[location:pittsburgh]].
