"I Apologize" is a 1948 ballad recording by [[person:billy-eckstine]] that became one of his signature MGM-era hits. Eckstine's burnished baritone glides over a lush string-and-horn arrangement, modeling the polished vocal style that anticipated the postwar crooner era. Related: [[location:pittsburgh]].
