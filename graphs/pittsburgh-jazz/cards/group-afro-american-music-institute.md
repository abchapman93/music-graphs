The Afro-American Music Institute (AAMI) is a Pittsburgh community music school founded in 1982 by [[person:dr-james-t-johnson-jr|Dr. James T. Johnson Jr.]] and [[person:pamela-johnson|Pamela Johnson]] to teach jazz, gospel, and African-rooted music to young Pittsburghers. AAMI is where Alec first learned to play jazz.
