Joe Negri is a guitarist born in [[location:pittsburgh]] in 1926 whose six-decade career as a bandleader and educator made him one of the city's defining mainstream-jazz voices. Nationally, he is best known as "Handyman Negri" on *Mister Rogers' Neighborhood*, where he appeared as a recurring on-screen friend of Fred Rogers from the 1960s through the 2001 series finale — often playing live alongside [[person:johnny-costa]].
