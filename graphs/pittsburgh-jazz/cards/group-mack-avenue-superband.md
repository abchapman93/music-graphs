The Mack Avenue SuperBand is a recurring all-star ensemble drawn from the Mack Avenue Records jazz roster, convened annually for the Detroit Jazz Festival and a series of live releases. [[person:sean-jones]] is a longtime member and composer for the band, including the Pittsburgh-themed composition "Liberty Avenue Stroll." Related: [[location:pittsburgh]].
