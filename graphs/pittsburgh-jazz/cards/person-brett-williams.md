Brett Williams is a Pittsburgh-rooted jazz pianist and keyboardist whose work spans modern post-bop, R&B, and contemporary instrumental music. He toured with bassist [[person:marcus-miller|Marcus Miller]] while in college and is a core member of the contemporary Pittsburgh collective [[group:elevations|Elevations]]. He is married to vocalist [[person:kate-k-s|Kate K-S]].
