"Roll 'em" is a boogie-woogie blues composed by [[person:mary-lou-williams]] in 1937 for Benny Goodman's orchestra. Williams's own piano recordings of the tune showcase the driving left-hand stride and bluesy right-hand fills that made her a foundational voice in swing-era piano. Related: [[location:pittsburgh]].
