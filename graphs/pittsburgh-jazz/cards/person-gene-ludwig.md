Gene Ludwig was a Hammond B-3 organist born in [[location:pittsburgh]] in 1937 and a fixture of the city's organ-trio scene for over five decades. He toured with Sonny Stitt and Lou Donaldson in the 1960s, then anchored long-running Pittsburgh organ trios that kept the soul-jazz tradition alive locally until his death in 2010.
