"Dad" is a track on [[person:roger-humphries|Roger Humphries]]'s album [[album:keep-the-faith|Keep the Faith]], written by drummer [[person:george-heid-iii|George Heid III]] and dedicated to his father, [[person:george-heid-ii|George Heid II]]. The track features pianist [[person:brett-williams|Brett Williams]].
