Benny Green is a pianist born in New York in 1963 and raised in Berkeley, California, whose hard-bop-rooted style was forged in stints with [[person:art-blakey]]'s Jazz Messengers (1987–1989) and the [[person:ray-brown]] Trio (1992–1997) — both anchored in [[location:pittsburgh]]-rooted bandleaders. He has led his own trios on Blue Note and Telarc since the late 1980s.
