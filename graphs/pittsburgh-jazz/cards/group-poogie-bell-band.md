The Poogie Bell Band is the leader project of drummer Poogie Bell — born in [[location:pittsburgh]] and a longtime collaborator with Marcus Miller, David Sanborn, and Erykah Badu. The band fuses jazz, funk, gospel, and R&B in a contemporary groove-jazz idiom rooted in Bell's Pittsburgh drumming pedigree. Related: [[location:pittsburgh]].
