Elevations is a contemporary Pittsburgh-based jazz collective drawing on hip-hop, neo-soul, and post-bop traditions. The group features vocalists Nathan Bellott and [[person:carolyn-perteete|Carolyn Perteete]] and emcee Dahi Divine across its leader recordings, representing the modern multi-genre face of the city's jazz scene. Related: [[location:pittsburgh]].

Core members include pianist [[person:brett-williams|Brett Williams]], bassist [[person:anton-defade|Anton Defade]], drummer [[person:george-heid-iii|George Heid III]], and trumpeter/vocalist [[person:benny-bennack-iii|Benny Bennack III]].
