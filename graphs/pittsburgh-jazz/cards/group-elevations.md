Elevations is a contemporary Pittsburgh-based jazz collective drawing on hip-hop, neo-soul, and post-bop traditions. The group features vocalists Nathan Bellott and Carolyn Perteete and emcee Dahi Divine across its leader recordings, representing the modern multi-genre face of the city's jazz scene.
