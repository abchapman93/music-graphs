Jimmy Ponder was a guitarist born in [[location:pittsburgh]] in 1946 whose warm, bluesy single-line style anchored a long career in soul-jazz and organ-trio settings. He recorded with Charles Earland, Lou Donaldson, and Stanley Turrentine before leading his own dates on Muse, LRC, and HighNote into the 2010s, remaining based in Pittsburgh for most of his life.
