"Cool Struttin'" is the title track of [[person:sonny-clark]]'s 1958 Blue Note album, a swaggering medium-tempo blues that opens with one of the most-quoted piano vamps in hard bop. Art Farmer, Jackie McLean, [[person:paul-chambers]], and Philly Joe Jones complete the quintet. Related: [[location:pittsburgh]].
