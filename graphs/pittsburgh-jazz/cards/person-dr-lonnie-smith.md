Dr. Lonnie Smith was a Hammond B-3 organist born in Lackawanna, New York in 1942 whose long career on Blue Note (initially as a sideman with [[person:george-benson]] and Lou Donaldson) helped define the soul-jazz organ-trio sound. His connection to [[location:pittsburgh]] is through Benson, whom he played with in the mid-1960s before launching his own leader records.
