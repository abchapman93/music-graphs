*No Need for Words* (2011, Mack Avenue) is trumpeter [[person:sean-jones|Sean Jones]]'s sixth album as a leader, featuring his working quartet with pianist Orrin Evans. The record's title essay (written by Jones) frames the album as a meditation on the limits of language and the directness of instrumental jazz.
