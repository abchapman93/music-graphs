"Us Three" is the title track of [[person:horace-parlan]]'s 1960 Blue Note debut as a leader — a trio session with George Tucker on bass and Al Harewood on drums — and the definitive showcase of Parlan's percussive, left-hand-led approach. Related: [[location:pittsburgh]].
