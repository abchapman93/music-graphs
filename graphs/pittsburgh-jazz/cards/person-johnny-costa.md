Johnny Costa was a pianist born in Arnold, Pennsylvania (near [[location:pittsburgh]]) in 1922 whose virtuosic Art Tatum–influenced technique made him a fixture of the Pittsburgh jazz scene. Nationally, he is best known as the on-air music director and improvising pianist for *Mister Rogers' Neighborhood* from 1968 until his death in 1996 — playing every cue live on a Baldwin grand.
