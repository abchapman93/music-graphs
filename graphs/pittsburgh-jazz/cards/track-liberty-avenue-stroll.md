"Liberty Avenue Stroll" is a [[person:sean-jones]] composition recorded by the [[group:mack-avenue-superband]], its title referencing Liberty Avenue — one of [[location:pittsburgh]]'s major downtown thoroughfares that runs from the Strip District through downtown and out into Bloomfield and Lawrenceville. See [[note:liberty-avenue]] for background on the composition.
