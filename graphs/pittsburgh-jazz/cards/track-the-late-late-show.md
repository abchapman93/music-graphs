"The Late, Late Show" is the title track of [[person:dakota-staton]]'s breakthrough 1957 Capitol album, a smoky after-hours ballad whose mood and phrasing made it a defining vocal-jazz hit of the late 1950s. Related: [[location:pittsburgh]].
