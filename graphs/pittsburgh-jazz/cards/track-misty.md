"Misty" is a jazz standard composed by [[person:erroll-garner]] in 1954, with lyrics later added by Johnny Burke. Garner's instrumental version was first released on his 1955 album *Contrasts*; the song became an enduring hit for Johnny Mathis in 1959 and has since been recorded by hundreds of artists, including Sarah Vaughan, Ella Fitzgerald, and Frank Sinatra. Its melodic warmth and graceful chord movement make it one of the most-played ballads in the American songbook.
