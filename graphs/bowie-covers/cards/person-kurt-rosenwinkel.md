Kurt Rosenwinkel (born 1970) is an American jazz guitarist whose fluid, harmonically rich style has been one of the most influential in contemporary jazz guitar. He appears alongside [[person:orrin-evans]] and [[person:kevin-eubanks]] on [[album:knowingishalfthebattle]], which includes a jazz interpretation of [[person:david-bowie]]'s [[song:kooks]].
