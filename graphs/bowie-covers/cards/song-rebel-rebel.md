"Rebel Rebel" is a song by [[person:david-bowie]], released on his 1974 album *Diamond Dogs* and as a single in many territories. Built on a famous chugging guitar riff that Bowie himself played, the song is often described as his farewell to the glam rock era and one of his catchiest, most directly rock-and-roll compositions. It has been covered by many artists; Brazilian singer-guitarist [[person:seu-jorge]] recorded a Portuguese-language acoustic version for Wes Anderson's 2004 film *The Life Aquatic with Steve Zissou*, included on the soundtrack album [[album:life-aquatic-studio-sessions]].
