*Ziggy Stardust* is a 1982 single by [[group:bauhaus]], a cover of [[person:david-bowie]]'s [[song:ziggy-stardust]]. Released as a standalone single between Bauhaus's studio albums *Mask* and *The Sky's Gone Out*, it became one of the most iconic cover recordings in post-punk history, defining the gothic subculture's relationship to Bowie through its transformation of the glam anthem into something darker and more austere.
