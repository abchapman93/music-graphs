Orrin Evans (born 1975) is a Philadelphia-based jazz pianist known for bold, rhythmically adventurous playing and a wide-ranging approach to material. His collaborative album [[album:knowingishalfthebattle]] with [[person:kurt-rosenwinkel]] and [[person:kevin-eubanks]] includes a jazz treatment of [[person:david-bowie]]'s [[song:kooks]], reimagining the gentle pop song as a loose, exploratory trio improvisation.
