"After All" is a 1970 [[person:david-bowie]] composition from *The Man Who Sold the World*, a waltz-time meditation on alienation and youth cults whose oblique lyrics ("Oh by jingo") and gothic atmosphere mark one of his earliest distinctly mature songs.
