"Starman" is a 1972 [[person:david-bowie]] composition from *The Ziggy Stardust* album, the lead single that introduced the Ziggy persona to a mass audience. Its octave-leap chorus — modeled on Judy Garland's "Over the Rainbow" — became a glam-rock signature.
