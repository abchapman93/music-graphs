"Ziggy Stardust" is a song written by [[person:david-bowie]], released in 1972 on *The Rise and Fall of Ziggy Stardust and the Spiders from Mars*. It describes the fictional rock star Ziggy Stardust, one of Bowie's most celebrated alter egos. [[group:bauhaus]] covered the song as [[album:ziggy-stardust-bauhaus-1982]], a 1982 single that turned the glam-rock anthem into a gothic post-punk dirge and became the defining Bowie cover of the early goth subculture.
