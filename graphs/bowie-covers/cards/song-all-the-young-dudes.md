"All the Young Dudes" is a song written by [[person:david-bowie]] and given as a gift to [[group:mott-the-hoople]], who recorded it as their signature hit in 1972. Bowie wrote and produced the recording after the band considered breaking up, transforming his anthemic glam-rock composition into one of the defining songs of the early 1970s British rock scene. The song appears on the album [[album:all-the-young-dudes-album]].
