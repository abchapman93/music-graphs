Brad Mehldau is an American jazz pianist renowned for his introspective trio recordings and his lifelong engagement with pop and rock repertoire. His Bowie interpretations carry the same quality he brings to Radiohead or the Beatles: a deep harmonic intelligence that finds the jazz latent in [[person:david-bowie]]'s songbook.
