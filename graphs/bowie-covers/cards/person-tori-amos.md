Tori Amos (b. 1963) is an American singer-songwriter and pianist whose 1992 debut *Little Earthquakes* established her as a defining voice of confessional piano-rock. Her 2001 covers album *Strange Little Girls* — songs written by men, reinterpreted from female perspectives — included her piano-led reading of [[person:david-bowie]]'s "After All".
