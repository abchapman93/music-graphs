Peter Gabriel (born 1950) is a British singer and songwriter, known first as the original lead vocalist of Genesis and then for a celebrated solo career. His 2010 album [[album:scratch-my-back]] was an orchestral covers project in which Gabriel reinterpreted songs by artists he admired — including [[person:david-bowie]]'s [[song:heroes]] — using only orchestral strings and voice, with no conventional pop instruments. Gabriel's reading of "Heroes" is among the most emotionally bare renditions of any Bowie song, stripping the recording to its melodic and lyrical core.
