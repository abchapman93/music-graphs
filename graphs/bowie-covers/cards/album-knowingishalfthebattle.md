*#knowingishalfthebattle* is a 2016 collaborative jazz album by [[person:orrin-evans]], [[person:kurt-rosenwinkel]], and [[person:kevin-eubanks]]. The album includes a freely improvised jazz treatment of [[person:david-bowie]]'s [[song:kooks]], from the [[album:hunky-dory]] album, taking the song's playful melodic material as a launching point for interplay between the three musicians.
