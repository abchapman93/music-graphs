"Let's Dance" is the 1983 title track of [[person:david-bowie]]'s Nile Rodgers-produced album, a chart-topping disco-funk single that gave Bowie his biggest mainstream commercial moment and featured a young Stevie Ray Vaughan on lead guitar.
