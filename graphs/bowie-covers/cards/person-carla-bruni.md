Carla Bruni (born 1967) is an Italian-French model and singer-songwriter who has released a series of intimate acoustic albums in the French chanson tradition. Her 2010 single [[album:absolute-beginners-carla-bruni-2010]] — a cover of [[person:david-bowie]]'s [[song:absolute-beginners]] — brings her characteristic delicacy and French pop sensibility to one of Bowie's most sweeping ballads.
