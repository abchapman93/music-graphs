"Space Oddity" is a song written by [[person:david-bowie]], released in 1969. Its tale of astronaut Major Tom floating alone in space established Bowie as a distinctive voice in British rock. [[person:cat-power]] recorded a spare, aching version on her 2022 covers album [[album:covers]], stripping the arrangement to near-silence around the melody.
