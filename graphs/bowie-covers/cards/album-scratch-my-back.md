*Scratch My Back* is a 2010 orchestral covers album by [[person:peter-gabriel]]. Gabriel reinterpreted songs by artists he admired using only orchestra and voice — no drums or guitars — including [[person:david-bowie]]'s [[song:heroes]]. The stark, stripped-down approach gives each song an unusual gravity; Gabriel's reading of "Heroes" is widely considered one of the finest cover versions of the song.
