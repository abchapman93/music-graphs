*These Are the Vistas* is the second studio album by [[group:bad-plus]] and their major-label debut, released in 2003 on Columbia Records. Produced by Tchad Blake, it broke the trio out of the jazz-club circuit through its committed, full-volume covers of rock songs alongside their original material. The album includes [[group:bad-plus]]'s reading of [[person:david-bowie]]'s [[song:life-on-mars]], as well as instrumental covers of Nirvana's "Smells Like Teen Spirit" and Blondie's "Heart of Glass." The mix of jazz piano-trio tradition, rock energy, and through-composed originals announced the band as a distinctive new voice in contemporary jazz.
