M. Ward (Matt Ward, b. 1973) is an American singer-songwriter and guitarist from Portland, Oregon, known for his dusty, fingerpicked folk-rock aesthetic and his work as half of She & Him with Zooey Deschanel. His covers — Bowie, Daniel Johnston, the Beach Boys — strip songs to acoustic essentials.
