*The Life Aquatic Studio Sessions Featuring Seu Jorge* is a 2005 album of [[person:seu-jorge]]'s acoustic, Portuguese-language covers of [[person:david-bowie]] songs, drawn from the recordings he made for Wes Anderson's 2004 film *The Life Aquatic with Steve Zissou*. The album collects fourteen Bowie covers, including [[song:rebel-rebel]], "Starman," "Life on Mars?," "Changes," "Five Years," "Lady Stardust," "Suffragette City," and "Ziggy Stardust." Bowie was a fan of Jorge's reinterpretations and gave them his blessing.
