"Heroes" is a song written by [[person:david-bowie]] and Brian Eno, released on Bowie's 1977 album of the same name. One of Bowie's most celebrated compositions, it draws on the Berlin Wall setting and the ambient-electronic sound world Bowie and Eno were developing together in Berlin. The song has proved unusually generative: [[person:philip-glass]] adapted the entire *Heroes* album as [[album:symphony-no-4-heroes]], while [[person:peter-gabriel]] recorded a stark orchestral cover on [[album:scratch-my-back]].
