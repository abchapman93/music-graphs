*Blackstar* (stylized as ★) is the twenty-fifth and final studio album by [[person:david-bowie]], released on January 8, 2016 — his 69th birthday, and two days before his death from liver cancer. Recorded in secret with a New York jazz quartet led by saxophonist Donny McCaslin, the album is harmonically and rhythmically more adventurous than anything he had released in decades. Songs like the title track, "Lazarus," and "I Can't Give Everything Away" were widely recognized after his death as an extraordinary, deliberate farewell. The album won five Grammy Awards in 2017, including Best Rock Performance and Best Rock Song for the title track.
