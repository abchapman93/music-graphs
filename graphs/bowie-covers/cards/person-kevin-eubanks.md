Kevin Eubanks (born 1957) is an American jazz guitarist best known as the longtime bandleader for *The Tonight Show with Jay Leno*. His collaborations outside that context include [[album:knowingishalfthebattle]] with [[person:orrin-evans]] and [[person:kurt-rosenwinkel]], a free-ranging session that includes a jazz reading of [[person:david-bowie]]'s [[song:kooks]].
