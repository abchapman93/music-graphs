*Absolute Beginners* is a 2010 single by [[person:carla-bruni]], a cover of [[person:david-bowie]]'s [[song:absolute-beginners]]. Bruni's version brings a spare, chanson-influenced arrangement that reimagines the soaring 1986 ballad as an intimate, understated recording.
