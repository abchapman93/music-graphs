Lulu (Marie Lawrie, b. 1948) is a Scottish singer and television presenter whose 1960s hits (*Shout*, *To Sir With Love*) made her one of the era's biggest British pop voices. Her 1974 [[person:david-bowie]]-produced cover of "The Man Who Sold the World" reached UK #3, the first major mainstream chart success for a Bowie composition.
