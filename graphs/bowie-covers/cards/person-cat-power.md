Cat Power is the stage name of Chan Marshall (born 1972), an American singer and songwriter known for emotionally raw, minimalist recordings. Her 2022 album [[album:covers]] includes a spare, haunting version of [[person:david-bowie]]'s [[song:space-oddity]] that exemplifies her approach to cover material: stripping songs of their production and finding the exposed lyrical core.
