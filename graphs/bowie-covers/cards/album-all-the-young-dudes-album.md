*All the Young Dudes* is the fifth studio album by [[group:mott-the-hoople]], released in 1972 and produced by [[person:david-bowie]]. Bowie wrote the title track [[song:all-the-young-dudes]] specifically for the band, and his production gave Mott the commercial breakthrough they had been unable to achieve on their own. The album defined the band's peak period and remains one of the key documents of early British glam rock.
