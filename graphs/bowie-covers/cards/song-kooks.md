"Kooks" is a song written by [[person:david-bowie]] and released on his 1971 album [[album:hunky-dory]]. A playful, affectionate song Bowie wrote for his newborn son, it has a deceptive lightness that rewards reinterpretation. The trio of [[person:orrin-evans]], [[person:kurt-rosenwinkel]], and [[person:kevin-eubanks]] recorded a jazz version on their collaborative album [[album:knowingishalfthebattle]].
