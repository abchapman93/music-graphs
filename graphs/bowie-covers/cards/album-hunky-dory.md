*Hunky Dory* is the fourth studio album by [[person:david-bowie]], released in December 1971. The first record with the band that would become The Spiders from Mars (Mick Ronson, Trevor Bolder, Mick Woodmansey), it features some of Bowie's most enduring songs, including [[song:life-on-mars]], "Changes," "Oh! You Pretty Things," and "Quicksand." The album's openly literate, piano-driven songwriting marked a major turning point in his career and set the stage for *The Rise and Fall of Ziggy Stardust and the Spiders from Mars* the following year. It is regularly cited as one of the greatest albums of the 1970s.
