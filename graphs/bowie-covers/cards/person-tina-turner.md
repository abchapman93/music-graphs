Tina Turner (1939–2023) was an American-born singer whose powerhouse voice and stage presence made her one of the great soul-rock vocalists of the 20th century. Her 1984 solo comeback (*Private Dancer*) coincided with her [[person:david-bowie]] collaboration on "Tonight" and her cover of his "1984" for the film soundtrack.
