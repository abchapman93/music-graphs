*Symphony No. 4 "Heroes"* is a 1996 orchestral composition by [[person:philip-glass]], adapted from [[person:david-bowie]] and Brian Eno's 1977 album *Heroes*. Glass transforms the album's electronic, ambient-rock soundscapes into a full symphonic work, demonstrating the structural and melodic depth underlying [[song:heroes]] and its companion pieces. The symphony is the second in Glass's trilogy of orchestral works adapted from Bowie's Berlin trilogy albums.
