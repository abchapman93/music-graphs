"Absolute Beginners" is a song written by [[person:david-bowie]], released in 1986 as part of the soundtrack for the film *Absolute Beginners*. One of Bowie's most sweeping ballads, it reached number two in the UK. [[person:carla-bruni]] recorded a delicate, French-inflected version in 2010 that became one of the more striking pop-chanson reinterpretations of any Bowie song.
