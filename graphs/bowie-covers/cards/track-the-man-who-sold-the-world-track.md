[[person:lulu]]'s 1974 cover of [[song:the-man-who-sold-the-world]], produced by [[person:david-bowie]] himself and featuring Bowie on saxophone. The single reached UK #3, becoming the first major mainstream chart success for a Bowie composition and a template for how the songbook could travel beyond his own recordings.
