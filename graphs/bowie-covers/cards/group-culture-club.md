Culture Club is a British new-wave band formed in London in 1981, fronted by Boy George with Mikey Craig, Roy Hay, and Jon Moss. Their 1980s run of hits (*Karma Chameleon*, *Do You Really Want to Hurt Me*) made them one of the era's defining pop-soul acts and a cousin to Bowie's gender-bending glam pop.
