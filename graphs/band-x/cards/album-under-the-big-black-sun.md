*Under the Big Black Sun* is the third studio album by [[group:x]], released in 1982 and again produced by Ray Manzarek. Written in the wake of the death of [[person:exene-cervenka]]'s sister Mary in a car accident, it is the band's darkest and most country-inflected record to date, drawing on the rockabilly, blues, and country influences that [[person:billy-zoom]] and [[person:dj-bonebrake]] had always brought to the band. Songs like "Riding with Mary," "Come Back to Me," and "The Have Nots" trade some of the earlier records' velocity for emotional depth without losing the harmonic edge of the [[person:john-doe]] / Cervenka vocal pairing.
