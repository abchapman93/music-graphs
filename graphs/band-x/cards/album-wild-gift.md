*Wild Gift* is the second album by [[group:x]], released in 1981 and again produced by Ray Manzarek. Coming barely a year after [[album:los-angeles]], it is often considered the band's peak: tighter playing, sharper songwriting, and the [[person:john-doe]] / [[person:exene-cervenka]] harmonies pushed even further into beautiful dissonance. Highlights include "The Once Over Twice," "We're Desperate," "White Girl," "In This House That I Call Home," and "The World's a Mess; It's in My Kiss." Multiple critics named it the best album of 1981.
