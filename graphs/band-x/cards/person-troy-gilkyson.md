Tony Gilkyson is an American guitarist and songwriter who served as the lead guitarist of [[group:x]] from 1986 to 1995, succeeding [[person:dave-alvin]] in the role and replacing original guitarist [[person:billy-zoom]] long-term. Son of folk singer Terry Gilkyson (composer of "The Bare Necessities"), he came to X with a background in West Coast roots rock, having worked extensively with Lone Justice. He played on the X albums *See How We Are* (1987), *Live at the Whisky a Go-Go* (1988), and *Hey Zeus!* (1993). After the original X lineup reunited in the late 1990s with Billy Zoom returning on guitar, Gilkyson continued working as a session musician, producer, and sideman with [[person:exene-cervenka]] and [[person:john-doe]] on various solo projects, as well as with a wide range of other Americana and country-rock artists out of Los Angeles. He represents the bridge era of X — the back half of the 1980s and the early 1990s — between Billy Zoom's two long stints with the band.
