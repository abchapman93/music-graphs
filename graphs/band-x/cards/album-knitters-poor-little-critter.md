*Poor Little Critter on the Road* is the 1985 debut album by The Knitters, the acoustic country-folk side project of [[group:x]]. The lineup features [[person:john-doe]] on vocals and acoustic bass, [[person:exene-cervenka]] on vocals, [[person:dj-bonebrake]] on drums and percussion, and [[person:dave-alvin]] on guitar, with Jonny Ray Bartel on upright bass. The album mixes original songs in a traditional country and rockabilly idiom with covers of Merle Haggard and other classic country and folk material. It illustrates how deeply the country and Americana influences ran in X from the beginning, and it laid the groundwork for the solo Americana careers that John Doe, Cervenka, and Alvin would pursue in the decades that followed.
