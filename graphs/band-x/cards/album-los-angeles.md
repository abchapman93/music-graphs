*Los Angeles* is the 1980 debut album by [[group:x]], produced by Ray Manzarek of The Doors and released on Slash Records. Recorded quickly and cheaply, it captures the band at the height of its early club-show energy: [[person:exene-cervenka]] and [[person:john-doe]] trading harsh, intertwined vocals over [[person:billy-zoom]]'s rockabilly-derived guitar and [[person:dj-bonebrake]]'s hard-swinging drumming. The album opens with the manic "Your Phone's Off the Hook, But You're Not" and includes the title track, "Sex and Dying in High Society," "Nausea," and a snarling cover of The Doors' "Soul Kitchen." It was widely praised on release and is now regularly cited on lists of the greatest punk albums.
