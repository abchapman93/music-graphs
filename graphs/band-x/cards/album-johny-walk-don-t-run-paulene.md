*Johny Walk Don't Run Paulene* (2001) is a Billy Zoom solo release, an instrumental record built around his signature clean Gretsch tone and his rockabilly and surf-rock vocabulary. It dates from the period between X's late-1990s reunion and Zoom's return to the band's road lineup in the 2000s.
