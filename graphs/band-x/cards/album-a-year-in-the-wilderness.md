*A Year in the Wilderness* (2007) is John Doe's fifth solo studio album, released on Yep Roc Records with guest appearances by [[person:dave-alvin]], Cindy Wasserman, Kathleen Edwards, and others. The songs lean into Americana and country-rock — a continuation of Doe's post-X songwriting voice that draws on the country and folk threads he and Cervenka pulled into X from the start.
