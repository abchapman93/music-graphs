*Somewhere Gone* (2009) is Exene Cervenka's first solo album in over a decade, released by Bloodshot Records. The record's stripped-down, country-inflected production foregrounds her voice and lyrics; the songs deal with mortality, Los Angeles, and political fatigue. It is widely regarded as her artistic high-water mark outside of X and the Knitters.
