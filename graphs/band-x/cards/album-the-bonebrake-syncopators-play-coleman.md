*The Bonebrake Syncopators Play Coleman* (2012) is a side-project record by DJ Bonebrake's vibraphone-led acoustic jazz group, the Bonebrake Syncopators. It works through Ornette Coleman's early compositional book via small-group jazz arrangements rather than free-jazz interpretation, foregrounding the melodies. Bonebrake plays vibes throughout — a distance from his work behind the X kit.
