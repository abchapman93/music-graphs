American musician, founder and frontman of Nine Inch Nails. With Atticus Ross, has scored films for David Fincher (The Social Network, The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo, Gone Girl, Mank, The Killer), Pixar (Soul), and others. Won Academy Awards for The Social Network (2011) and Soul (2021).
