English musician, lead guitarist of Radiohead, and prolific film composer. Long-time scoring collaborator with Paul Thomas Anderson (There Will Be Blood, The Master, Phantom Thread, Licorice Pizza) and frequent partner of Lynne Ramsay (We Need to Talk About Kevin, You Were Never Really Here) and Pablo Larraín (Spencer).
