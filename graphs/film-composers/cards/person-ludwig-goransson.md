Swedish composer, conductor, and producer known for his film scores for Ryan Coogler (Fruitvale Station, Creed, Black Panther, Sinner) and Christopher Nolan (Tenet, Oppenheimer), as well as his work with Childish Gambino. Won the Academy Award for Best Original Score for Oppenheimer (2024).
