A graph centered on Miles Davis, the era-defining albums he led, and the sidemen who passed through his bands. The first round covers the pivotal Davis-led albums — *Birth of the Cool*, the first-quintet Prestige sessions (*Relaxin'*, *Workin'*, *Steamin'*, *Cookin'*), *Kind of Blue*, the second-quintet Plugged Nickel run, *In a Silent Way*, *Bitches Brew*, *On the Corner*, and *Tutu* — and the personnel who appeared on them. The second round expands on the sidemen who went on to lead their own influential records as bandleaders or solo artists: John Coltrane, Bill Evans, Cannonball Adderley, Herbie Hancock, Wayne Shorter, Chick Corea, and others.
