American pianist and keyboardist, the harmonic anchor of [[person:miles-davis]]'s second great quintet (1963-1968) and a key voice on *In a Silent Way* and *Bitches Brew*. As a leader he recorded *Maiden Voyage* (1965), the Mwandishi sextet records, and the funk-jazz landmark *Head Hunters* (1973).
