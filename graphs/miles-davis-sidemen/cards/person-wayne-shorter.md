American tenor and soprano saxophonist and composer who wrote much of the second-quintet book for [[person:miles-davis]] (1964-1970) and stayed through *Bitches Brew*. Led a string of Blue Note classics (*Speak No Evil*, *JuJu*) and co-founded Weather Report with [[person:miles-davis]] alumnus Joe Zawinul.
