American hard-bop pianist who replaced [[person:red-garland]] in [[person:miles-davis]]'s rhythm section in 1959 — he plays on "Freddie Freeloader" on *Kind of Blue* and stayed through *Someday My Prince Will Come*. Later led a trio with [[person:paul-chambers]] and [[person:jimmy-cobb]] that backed Wes Montgomery on the landmark *Smokin' at the Half Note*.
