American alto saxophonist who played alongside [[person:john-coltrane]] in [[person:miles-davis]]'s sextet on *Milestones* (1958) and *Kind of Blue* (1959), then led his own hard-bop and soul-jazz groups, scoring a hit with *Mercy, Mercy, Mercy!* (1966).

Cannonball Adderley appears on [[album:milestones|Milestones]].

Cannonball Adderley appears on [[album:kind-of-blue|Kind of Blue]].

Cannonball Adderley released [[album:somethin-else|Somethin' Else]].

Cannonball Adderley released [[album:mercy-mercy-mercy|Mercy, Mercy, Mercy!]].

Cannonball Adderley released [[album:the-cannonball-adderley-quintet-in-san-francisco|The Cannonball Adderley Quintet in San Francisco]].
