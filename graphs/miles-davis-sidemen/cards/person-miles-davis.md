American trumpeter, bandleader, and composer whose career across five decades repeatedly redefined jazz — from bebop and cool jazz in the late 1940s and 1950s, through modal jazz on *Kind of Blue* (1959), the second-quintet adventurism of the mid-1960s, the electric fusion of *In a Silent Way* and *Bitches Brew*, the dense funk of *On the Corner*, and the 1980s synth-pop jazz of *Tutu*. Many of the players he hired as sidemen went on to lead their own genre-defining bands.
