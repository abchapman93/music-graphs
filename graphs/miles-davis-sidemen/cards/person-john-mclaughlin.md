English guitarist whose volume, distortion, and modal vocabulary helped define [[person:miles-davis]]'s electric period on *In a Silent Way*, *Bitches Brew*, *A Tribute to Jack Johnson*, and *On the Corner*. Later led the Mahavishnu Orchestra.
