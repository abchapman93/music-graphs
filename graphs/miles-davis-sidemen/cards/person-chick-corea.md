American pianist and keyboardist who joined [[person:miles-davis]]'s electric band in 1968 and played on *In a Silent Way*, *Bitches Brew*, and *Live-Evil*. Led the acoustic quartet behind *Now He Sings, Now He Sobs* (1968) and the fusion group Return to Forever.
