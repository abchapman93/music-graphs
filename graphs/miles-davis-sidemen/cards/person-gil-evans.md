Canadian-American arranger and bandleader whose orchestral collaborations with [[person:miles-davis]] — *Miles Ahead*, *Porgy and Bess*, *Sketches of Spain*, *Quiet Nights* — defined a strand of large-ensemble jazz writing distinct from Ellington or the big-band tradition.
