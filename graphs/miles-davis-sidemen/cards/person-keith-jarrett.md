American pianist who played electric keyboards in [[person:miles-davis]]'s early-1970s band (heard on *Live-Evil*, *Get Up with It*) before becoming the defining solo-improviser pianist of his generation through ECM (*The Köln Concert*, the standards trio with [[person:jack-dejohnette]]).
