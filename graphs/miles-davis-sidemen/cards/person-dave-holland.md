English bassist who joined [[person:miles-davis]]'s electric band in 1968, playing on *Filles de Kilimanjaro*, *In a Silent Way*, *Bitches Brew*, and *A Tribute to Jack Johnson*. Built a major leader career on ECM thereafter.
