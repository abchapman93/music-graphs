American pianist whose impressionistic harmonic vocabulary shaped *Kind of Blue* with [[person:miles-davis]] in 1959. His own trio recordings, especially the 1961 Village Vanguard sessions with Scott LaFaro, became the template for the modern jazz piano trio.

Bill Evans appears on [[album:kind-of-blue|Kind of Blue]].

Bill Evans released [[album:portrait-in-jazz|Portrait in Jazz]].

Bill Evans released [[album:sunday-at-the-village-vanguard|Sunday at the Village Vanguard]].

Bill Evans released [[album:waltz-for-debby|Waltz for Debby]].

Bill Evans released [[album:conversations-with-myself|Conversations with Myself]].
