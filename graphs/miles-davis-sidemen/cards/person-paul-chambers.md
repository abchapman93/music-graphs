American bassist who anchored the rhythm section on [[person:miles-davis]]'s late-1950s recordings — the four Prestige Quintet sessions, *'Round About Midnight*, *Milestones*, and *Kind of Blue* — bringing a singing tone and consistently inventive walking lines.
