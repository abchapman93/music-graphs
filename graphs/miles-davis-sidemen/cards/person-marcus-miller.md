American bassist, producer, and multi-instrumentalist who wrote, produced, and played most of the instruments on [[person:miles-davis]]'s *Tutu* (1986) and *Amandla* (1989), shaping the synth-pop-jazz sound of Davis's late-period Warner Bros. records.
