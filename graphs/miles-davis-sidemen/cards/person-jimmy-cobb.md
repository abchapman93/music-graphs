American drummer who replaced [[person:philly-joe-jones]] in [[person:miles-davis]]'s rhythm section in 1958 and played on *Kind of Blue* (1959), *Sketches of Spain* (1960), and *Someday My Prince Will Come* (1961). The last surviving member of the *Kind of Blue* sessions, he continued leading and touring well into the 21st century.
