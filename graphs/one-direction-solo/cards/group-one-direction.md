English-Irish pop group formed on *The X Factor UK* in 2010, comprising [[person:harry-styles]], [[person:niall-horan]], [[person:liam-payne]], [[person:louis-tomlinson]], and [[person:zayn-malik]]. Released five studio albums between 2011 and 2015 before going on indefinite hiatus in 2016. Zayn departed in March 2016 ahead of the hiatus.

Members: [[person:harry-styles]], [[person:niall-horan]], [[person:liam-payne]], [[person:louis-tomlinson]], [[person:zayn-malik]].
