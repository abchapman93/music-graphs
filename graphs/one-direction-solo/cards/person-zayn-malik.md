English singer-songwriter and former member of [[group:one-direction]] (departed March 2016). First to launch a solo career, with *Mind of Mine* (2016), followed by *Icarus Falls* (2018), *Nobody Is Listening* (2021), and *Room Under the Stairs* (2024), moving from alternative R&B toward acoustic Americana.
